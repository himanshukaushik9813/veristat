# Veristat Neutrality Policy

This policy is published before anything else Veristat launches, and it outranks
every commercial consideration.

1. **Providers can never pay to change a score.** Under any framing. Ever.

2. **Providers may pay for increased probe frequency** — more data, faster
   confidence — and this is disclosed on their scorecard.

3. **Any provider can contest a score.** Contested results trigger a re-probe
   with fresh wallets. The dispute and its outcome are published regardless of
   result.

4. **Veristat's own ASP listing is scored by the same methodology**, displayed
   with an explicit conflict-of-interest label.

5. **Every published claim is factual and evidence-linked** — "returned X when
   the contract read Y at block N" — never editorial characterization.
   Veristat publishes measurements, not accusations.

6. **No token.** The credibility position depends on it.
